Lasagna Recipe

This is the best classic lasagna recipe

It is the perfect comfort food meal!

There's something so amazing about homemade, made-from-scratch lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Layers of gooey melted mozzarella cheese
  • A parmesan ricotta cheese
  • A robust tomato meat sauce

Steps

  1. Start by making a homemade meat sauce.
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture.
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles.
  4. Bring on the cheese!
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes
